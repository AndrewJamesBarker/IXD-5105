Creative Writing Graduate Certificate

About

The creative writing course is essentially a novel writing course where over 28 weeks, graduates from the Creative Writing - Fiction, Creative Non-Fiction and Poetry program work with a writer-mentor to improve their book-length projects. Through this diploma in creative writing, students learn how to build plots, characters, themes and storylines.

Through HumberCurrent, the creative writing alumni can share their creative works with the world. Below is an extensive list of our alumni and a little about their lives and their work. Scroll to learn more and connect.
